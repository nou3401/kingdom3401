Kingdom Days

Preparation creates victory.

Kingdom 3401 values daily activity, preparation, and strong participation both during off-season and KvK.

Off-Season

Each player earns crystal chests by destroying 60 fortresses per week. Daily Blue A is maintained, kingdom buffs are actively used, and players participate in events such as Alliance Mobilization.

KvK

A quota is set during KvK. Only excellent warriors who meet kingdom standards and contribute actively can be members.

From Aziz to Bodda, the kingdom marched on.
